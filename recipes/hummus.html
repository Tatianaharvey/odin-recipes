Roasted Beet Hummus

Can hummus get anymore beautiful then this dish?

A delicious blend of lemon, garlic and olive oil, with the colorful and nourishing aspects of beets, really step up this hummus dish to be the ultimate appetizer or snack!

Pair it with chopped up fresh vegetables, such as cucumbers, peppers, and carrots, or spread it on toast with fresh, sliced avocado for an outstanding open-faced sandwich! And don't worry to my fellow millenials out there, you won't break the bank with this avocado and hummus toast.

I hope you enjoy this creamy, lemon and garlicky goodness!

Ingredients

  • 1 small roasted beet
  • 1 15oz can cooked chickpeas, mostly drained
  • 1 large lemon, zested
  • 1/2 large lemon juiced
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper
  • 2 large cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 heaping tbsp tahini
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions

  1. Once the beet is cooled and peeled after roasting, quarter is and place it in your food processor. Blend until only small bits remain.
  2. Add remaining ingredients except olive oil and blend until smooth.
  3. Drizzle in olive oil as the hummus is mixing
  4. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed, adding more salt, lemon juice, or olive if needed. If too thick, add a bit of water.
  5. This hummus will keep in fridge for up to a week.
