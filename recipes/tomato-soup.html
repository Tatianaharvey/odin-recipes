Roasted Tomato and Pepper Soup

A fun summer lunch isn't complete without a delicious tomato soup!

Roasting the veggies for this soup adds even more flavour and paired with a grilled cheese sandwich will bring you right back to your childhood. The best part is, the flavors and ingredients blend together so nicely that you won't believe this comforting recipe is actually quite nourishing for you as well!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium sized heirloom tomatoes cut into small pieces
  • 2 bell peppers cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 onion sliced
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt or to taste
  • dash of sirracha (optional)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. roast the veggies for 25 minutes.
  2. Add to a blender with the rest of the soup ingredients and blend until a smooth consistency. heat in a pan on low heat for about 12 minutes or until the soup is completely warmed through.
  3. Make the grilled cheese on the stovetop and cook for a couple of minutes on each side until the cheese is melted and bread is golden brown.
  4. Serve soup in a bowl with either the grilled cheese on the side or cut into grilled cheese cubes to top your soup with.
  5. Enjoy!
