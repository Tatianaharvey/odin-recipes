Roasted Tomato and Pepper Soup

A fun summer lunch isn't complete without a delicious tomato soup!

Roasting the veggies for the soup adds even more flavour and pair it with a grilled cheese sandwich to be brought back to your childhood. The best part is, the flavors and ingredients blend together so nicely that you won't believe this comforting recipe is actually quite nourishing for you as well!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium sized heirloom tomatoes cut into small pieces
